# PROPOSAL: Glen Service Digital Portal
**Subject:** Digital Transformation & Maintenance for Glen Service Operations
**Date:** February 4, 2026

---

## 1. Executive Summary
This proposal outlines the deployment and ongoing management of the **Glen Service Portal**, a high-performance web application designed to streamline service ticket management, technician tracking, and operational reporting. By replacing legacy manual systems with a secure, real-time cloud architecture, Glen Service achieves superior data integrity and faster response times.

## 2. Core Features (Live)
*   **Technician Dashboard**: Seamless ticket management and real-time job status updates for field staff.
*   **Admin Control Panel**: Centralized view of all service tickets, attendance logs, and performance reports.
*   **Cloud Architecture**: Migrated to **Supabase** (Professional Relational Database) for instant data sync and maximum security.
*   **Secure Authentication**: Role-based access control for Technicians and Administrators.
*   **High-Speed Deployment**: Hosted on **Vercel** with global edge delivery for zero-lag user experience.

## 3. The Future Roadmap (Phase 2 Upscale)
To further dominate the local market and improve customer satisfaction, we propose the following upgrades:
*   **WhatsApp Automation**: Automated service confirmation and technician arrival alerts to customers.
*   **Digital Invoicing & Payments**: Generate and send payment links or invoices directly via the app.
*   **Google Reviews Booster**: Automated review requests sent to customers upon job completion to build online reputation.

## 5. Behind the Scenes: Real-World Value
To provide this level of performance and security, the system utilizes an enterprise-grade backend stack:
1.  **Database Storage**: High-frequency NVMe drives for instant data retrieval.
2.  **Security**: End-to-end SSL encryption and secure environment variable management (prevents data leaks).
3.  **Real-Time Sync**: Webhook-based architecture ensuring the system and backups (Google Sheets) stay in lock-step.
4.  **Edge Network**: Content delivery via 100+ global locations ensuring the app works perfectly even on slow mobile data.
